My Career Path

Lucas P Basso, born in Montevideo, Uruguay, began his musical career at the age of 14 when he joined Uruguay's most prominent Guns N' Roses tribute band, with which he opened for Dave Evans, ACDC's original lead singer.

Over time, he continued to study and work with various groups like Lobo Viejo, Guzmán Méndez, and collaborated with other artists. At the young age of 16, he released his first solo song, an exciting instrumental rock track that he produced himself.

At the age of 20, Lucas P Basso made the decision to move to Madrid, Spain, where he built a professional career as a guitarist, bassist, and producer. He went from playing on the streets of the city center to working with renowned artists such as The Liberty, Lydia Rodríguez (from Presuntos Implicados), La Factoría Orchestra, among others.

In addition to his previous collaborations and projects, Lucas P Basso created his own band, Kayzen, where he continues to showcase his musical skill and creativity alongside Adri Acero, Fabián Pérez Correa, and Leo Zombie.
